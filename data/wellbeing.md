Wellbeing Support

The university provides a range of wellbeing and support services for students.

Types of support include:
- Mental health and emotional wellbeing support
- Counselling services
- Disability and accessibility support
- Academic support and study help
- Financial and personal support

Accessing support:
- Students can explore support options through the wellbeing section of the university website.
- Many services require booking or contacting a support team directly.

Urgent support:
- If you are in immediate danger or at risk of harm, contact emergency services or urgent support channels.
