Student Support

The university provides a central student support hub for a wide range of questions and services.

General guidance:
- Students should search the university help pages for information about common issues.
- If the help pages do not answer the question, students can contact Nexus for support online, by phone, or on campus.
- Nexus is a general help point for student queries and can help direct students to the right service.

Examples of support areas include:
- Health and wellbeing support
- Safety and urgent support
- School support teams and study-related help
- Disability and neurodiversity support
- Financial support
- Cost of living support
- Student administration
- Immigration and visa support

Further support:
- Nexus online: https://help.kent.ac.uk
- Student support hub: https://student.kent.ac.uk/support

If a question is urgent or sensitive, students should use the appropriate official support or emergency channels.
