Late Submission and Penalties

If coursework is submitted after the deadline, penalties may apply.

General guidance:
- Work submitted up to 24 hours late may still be marked, but the maximum recorded mark may be capped.
- Work submitted between 24 and 48 hours late may still be marked, but the maximum mark may be limited to a pass.
- Work submitted more than 48 hours late may receive a mark of zero unless mitigation is approved.

Students should:
- Submit as soon as possible, even if late.
- Check their school’s specific regulations, as details may vary.

For official rules and details, students should refer to the university’s assessment regulations or support pages.
