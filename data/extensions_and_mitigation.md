Extensions and Mitigation

If a student is unable to meet a deadline due to exceptional circumstances, they may be able to request an extension or submit a mitigation claim.

Key points:
- Students should apply for mitigation as soon as possible.
- Requests may require supporting evidence.
- Approval is not guaranteed and depends on the circumstances.

Students should:
- Follow the university’s official mitigation or extension process.
- Contact their school or support services if they are unsure what to do.

If a deadline is missed without an approved extension or mitigation, late submission penalties may apply.
