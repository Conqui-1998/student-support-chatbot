Admissions and Offers

Applying:
- Undergraduate students apply through UCAS.
- Postgraduate students usually apply directly through the university.

Offers:
- If your application is successful, you will receive an offer.
- Undergraduate offers are confirmed through UCAS.
- Postgraduate offers are communicated through the university portal (e.g. KentVision).

Accepting an offer:
- Undergraduate students must select the university as their firm or insurance choice via UCAS.
- Postgraduate students accept offers through the university portal.

Conditions:
- If your offer is conditional, you must provide evidence that you have met the requirements before you can enroll.

Deferring entry:
- You can request to defer your entry, usually by up to one academic year, but this is not guaranteed.
