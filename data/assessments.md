Exams and Assessments

The university provides guidance and support for exams and assessments.

Key information:
- Exam timetables and details are usually provided through official university systems or student portals.
- Students should check their timetable regularly for updates.

During exams:
- Students are expected to follow university rules and regulations.
- Instructions for each exam will be provided in advance.

If something goes wrong:
- If you miss an exam or are affected by exceptional circumstances, you should contact your school or follow the university’s official procedures for reporting issues.

Support:
- Students can access support for exams and assessments through university support services or their academic school.

Further information:
