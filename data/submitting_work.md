Submitting Your Work

Most coursework is submitted online through the university’s learning platform (such as Moodle).

Key points:
- Each assignment will have a specified deadline.
- Deadlines are usually set at midday (12:00), but students should always check the specific details for their module.
- Submission instructions will be provided for each assignment.

Students should:
- Submit work before the deadline where possible.
- Check submission confirmation where available.
- Ensure they follow the correct format and instructions.

If unsure about submission requirements, students should check their module guidance or contact their school or academic support team.
